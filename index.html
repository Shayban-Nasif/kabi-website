Who is Kabi

A Story of Persistence

From a small village in Kushtia, Bangladesh to the data centers of Microsoft in Tokyo — Nasim's journey is one of relentless persistence, boundless curiosity, and creative fire.

He left home with a note in the drawer and a dream in his heart. Rejected by tech giants multiple times, he kept building, kept learning, kept writing. Today he works at Microsoft by day and weaves Bengali poetry by night.

His writing — poems, stories, songs — is a window into the soul of a man who carries two worlds: the warmth of Bangladesh and the precision of Japan.

The Journey

Life in Chapters

A life lived across two continents, three cultures, and countless stories.

Personal Early Life

Roots in Kushtia

Born and raised in a small village in Kushtia, Bangladesh. Found solace and expression in poetry and storytelling from an early age — a habit that never left him.

Education 2007

Secondary School Certificate

Completed SSC from Amla Sadarpur High School, Kushtia — the first step in a journey toward technology and the world beyond his village.

Professional Oct 2010 – Jan 2011

Technical Intern

REWM Project — First professional experience in technology, laying the groundwork for a lifelong career in IT.

Education 2011

Diploma in Computer Technology

Kushtia Polytechnic Institute — Built foundational skills in networking, hardware, and software systems.

Professional Apr 2012 – Dec 2017

Program Associate – Technical Support

CLP — Delivered IT support across 250 schools in all 64 districts of Bangladesh. Trained 1,200 high school teachers and 800 head teachers. Earned Outstanding Performer designation.

Personal 2012 – 2017

Exploring All 64 Districts

During his work at CLP, Nasim traveled to every single district in Bangladesh — experiencing the country's full breadth of culture, landscape, and humanity.

Education 2017

B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering

Bangladesh University — Completed his engineering degree while continuing professional IT work and nurturing his creative writing.

Professional 2018

IT Leadership Roles

Served as Assistant Director – IT Support (DNET) and Assistant Manager – IT Support (KITE), stepping into leadership within the tech sector.

Personal 2019

The Note in the Drawer

Left home for Japan with a handwritten note in the drawer and a dream in his heart — beginning the most transformative chapter of his life.

Professional Jan 2019 – Apr 2020

Machine Engineer – INOSYS

Japan — First role in Japan. Adapted to a new country, language, and work culture while building new technical competencies from scratch.

Personal 2019 – Present

Exploring Japan

Traveled across 22 of Japan's 47 prefectures, continuing his lifelong mission as an explorer — pen and camera always in hand.

Professional May 2020 – Dec 2025

NOC Engineer – NTT

Tokyo, Japan — Monitored global network operations and submarine cable systems, ensuring reliable global connectivity. Earned Remarkable Performer designation four times.

Personal 2019 – 2026

Featured in Media

Appeared in CLP Magazine (2019, p.21), DI Insider Life (pp.16 & 36), Rupali Bangladesh e-paper (2026), and Daily Punorutthan.

Professional Dec 2025 – Present

Data Center Technician – Microsoft

Tokyo, Japan — The dream realized. After being rejected in 2022, Nasim joined Microsoft's Tokyo data center team — proving that persistence, not perfection, wins.
